The Strong Lean Recipe

A physical guide to mixing 100 mL of codeine and promethazine syrup with precision.

1. The Codeine Base (100 mL)

Crush 40 Tablets (600mg Total)
  1. Crush: Grind 40 codeine tablets (15mg each) into a fine dust using the mortar.
  2. Dissolve: Pour in 50 mL of purified water. The powder should vanish instantly.
  3. Thicken: Add 50 mL of neutral corn syrup to create the 100 mL volume.
  4. Color: Add 1-2 drops of purple dye. The codeine will turn a deep violet.

2. The Promethazine Base (100 mL)

Crush 20 Tablets (500mg Total)
  1. Crush: Grind 20 promethazine tablets (25mg each).
  2. Dissolve: Mix into 50 mL purified water and 50 mL corn syrup.
  3. Stabilize: Promethazine is sticky; the corn syrup prevents separation.
  4. Color: Add 1-2 drops of the same purple dye to match the codeine.

3. The Final Potency (The Blend)

Combine into 200 mL Total
  1. Combine: Pour the codeine and promethazine into a larger, stoppered glass vessel.
  2. Ratio: You now have 600mg Codeine and 500mg Promethazine.
  3. Taste Test: The syrup should be smooth, slightly viscous, and intensely flavored.
  4. Duration: Store in the fridge. It stays potent for 2 weeks.

4. Storage & Maintenance

Keep it Pure
  • Use airtight glass bottles to prevent oxidation.
  • Keep away from direct sunlight to prevent the purple dye from fading.
  • Shake well before every dose to ensure the codeine stays suspended.
